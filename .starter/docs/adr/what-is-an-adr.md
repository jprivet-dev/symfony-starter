# What is an ADR (Architecture Decision Records)?

[⬅️ STARTER](../STARTER.md)

---

An **Architectural Decision Record (ADR)** is a short, plain text document that captures a **significant architectural decision**, its context, the options considered, and its consequences. It serves as a **log of the project's architectural journey**.

Its main purpose is to:

1. **Record the "Why"**: Explain the rationale behind a decision at a specific point in time, preventing future developers from questioning or re-litigating it without understanding the original constraints.
2. **Ensure consistency**: Maintain a shared understanding of the architecture across the team.
3. **Facilitate onboarding**: Help new team members quickly understand the project's foundational technical choices.
